🔏 Golden Data Pledge

Your trust is the main ingredient. At Yotta Palate Paradox, we protect your personal information like a treasured family recipe — with care, transparency, and no hidden spices.

🍯 1. Who we are & our cream-colored promise

Yotta Palate Paradox (referred to as "we", "us", or "our") operates the website yottapalate.com and all related culinary atelier services. We celebrate cream & yellow gastronomy, recipe sharing, and community warmth. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect any personal data you share with us when you visit our website, use our contact forms, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our golden content.

✨ core value We never sell your data. Your messages are handled like handwritten notes — personal, confidential, and replied with warmth.

📝 2. Information we may collect

We collect limited information to improve your experience and respond to your inquiries:

  • Identity & contact details: name, email address, any details you voluntarily provide in our contact form or via email correspondence.
  • Recipe / message content: the subject, recipe ideas, or golden notes you share with us (we keep these to understand our community's tastes).
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and interactions with our website (through anonymized analytics) to improve page performance. We do not use invasive tracking.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: only essential session cookies (no third-party marketing cookies). For details see our Cookies Policy.

✨ 3. How we use your data

We use your personal information only for legitimate purposes that align with our atelier's mission:

  • To respond to your questions, recipe stories, or collaboration requests sent via the contact form or email.
  • To send occasional updates about golden recipes, events, or Paradox announcements — only if you explicitly opt-in (we provide an unsubscribe link in every email).
  • To improve our website content and user experience based on general usage patterns.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., record-keeping for correspondence).
📬 Your golden note, our promise: When you message us via the contact form, we store your inquiry and email address only for the duration needed to assist you. We will never add you to a mailing list without your clear consent.

🔒 4. Data storage & security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against accidental loss, unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your information is stored on secure servers located in the United States, and we restrict access to team members who need it to provide you with service. We regularly review our data collection practices.

While no internet transmission is 100% secure, we use SSL encryption on our contact forms and keep retention periods minimal: contact form data is kept for no more than 24 months for internal reference unless you request earlier deletion.

📎 5. Sharing your information

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited scenarios:

  • Service providers: trusted third-party email platform (e.g., for newsletter delivery) which has signed data protection agreements and uses data solely for the services we request.
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
  • Business transfers: in the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, we will notify you and ensure your data remains protected under similar terms.

We do not integrate with social media trackers or advertising networks. Your interactions remain within our cream-hued corner of the internet.

🍪 6. Cookies & automated technologies

Our website uses minimal cookies that are strictly necessary for functionality (e.g., remembering your navigation between pages). These do not collect any identifiable information for marketing. For more details, please view our dedicated Cookies Policy. By using our website, you consent to these essential cookies.

👤 7. Your rights (GDPR & CCPA aligned)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): request deletion of your data where there is no compelling reason for continued processing.
  • Right to restrict processing: limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: obtain your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: object to direct marketing or processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please reach out via hello@yottapalate.com or use our contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

🧑‍🍳 8. Children's privacy

Our website and content are intended for general audiences and not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

🌍 9. International transfers

Our servers are based in the US. If you access our site from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US, where data protection laws may differ from your jurisdiction. By using our site, you consent to this transfer.

📅 10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom indicates when the latest revision took effect. We encourage you to review this page occasionally. Any material changes will be highlighted on our homepage or via direct communication where appropriate.
