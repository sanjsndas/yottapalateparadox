At Yotta Palate Paradox, we believe in complete transparency. Our website uses only minimal cookies that make your experience smooth — like warm butter on cream brioche. No tracking pixels, no ads, no surprises.

📜 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet or mobile) when you visit websites. They help remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them whenever you navigate between pages. We use only strictly necessary cookies that enable core functionality — like page routing and session consistency — without collecting any personal data for marketing or analytics platforms. Think of them as the 'silent sous-chefs', helpful and discreet.

🍯 2. Our cookie philosophy — the golden pledge

🌟 Zero third-party tracking. Zero advertising cookies. Zero analytics scripts that fingerprint you.
Unlike most websites, we don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any external trackers. The only minimal cookies we set are first-party, essential, and purely functional. Your visit stays in our cream-colored lounge — private and respectful.

🕹️ 4. Managing and disabling cookies

Since we only use essential and functional cookies, disabling them may affect your experience — for instance, the website might not remember your page preferences or the cookie banner might reappear. However, you have full control. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data → Block all cookies (selective blocking possible).
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Enhanced Tracking Protection → Custom → Cookies.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Block all cookies.
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies.

Please note that if you disable all cookies (including essential ones), some interactive elements of our website may not work properly, but you can always contact us directly via email for any assistance.
